Helping Hands For All Creatures Great And Small

Be a part of something big! Donate and get involved.

Vision

Awaken the world to the reality of GOD and HIS KINGDOM by being HIS loving hands and heart, which will serve people, animals, and nature.

Mission

We are GOD directed and we will glorify HIM by serving. We are here to create real transformation of the heart by fulfilling HIS will, expressed through love, compassion, and good works. In practice, this will be carried out by helping people in need, helping animals in need, and helping nature in need. We are here to encourage and lift up HIS Children so they can realize their full potential. We need to renew the Spiritual connections to each other, animals, and nature that have been severed by modern life. We do this, because we love HIM.

What We Do

We are Helping Hands For All Creatures Great And Small; a GOD directed nonprofit that is here to create real transformation of the heart. Our organization has a simple goal; to help people, animals, and nature in need. On a global scale. This will be achieved by working directly with individuals and collaborating with other nonprofits to accomplish amazing things.

It may sound like too big a task, but this is coming from the only ONE who can make anything happen. GOD HIMSELF!

What will your donation achieve?

  • It will help people in need, be it homeless families, single parents, orphans, mentally ill, and more.
  • We will create an animal rescue to help animals in need and find them loving homes.
  • We will also help nature by cleaning up public areas such as beaches, wild life areas, and communities.

But that's just the beginning. We will also raise funds for other nonprofits that are doing GOD's work. Specifically, nonprofits that are overlooked by public grants and other organizations.

If you're donating money, pray and ask GOD how much you should give. If you want to donate supplies, provide resources, and/or volunteer, please email us at serve@helpinghandsforall.net.

Let's come together and love our neighbors the way GOD intended, by being the Helping Hands for All Creatures Great And Small!
